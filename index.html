Cutthroat Trout

Cutthroat Trout are native to Colorado, as well as much of the western United States and Canada. This fish and its subspecies can be found in the Rio Grande, Green River, Colorado River, and more.

Greenback Cutthroat Trout

The Greenback Cutthroat Trout, once widespread in the Arkansas and South Platte Rivers of Eastern Colorado and southeast Wyoming, today occupies less than 1% of its historical range.



Colorado River Cutthroat Trout

The Colorado River Cutthroat Trout is a subspecies of cutthroat trout native only to the Green and Colorado River basins.
